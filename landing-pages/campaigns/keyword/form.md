# Superinsight — Landing Page Brief (`keyword/form.html`)

> Foundation doc for writing ads that match this landing page. Everything below is
> drawn from what the form actually says and does, so ad copy stays message-matched
> (better relevance, Quality Score, and conversion rate).

## 1. What Superinsight provides

**AI-powered medical record review & chronology software.** Superinsight ingests
large, messy medical record sets and returns structured, **page-cited** outputs in
about an hour:

- Medical record **reviews / summaries**
- Medical **chronologies / timelines**
- Key-evidence extraction (causation, treatment, inconsistencies, RFC, ratings, etc.)

**Core differentiators (use these in ads):**

- **AI only — no human ever reads the records.** Privacy-first by design.
- **Every finding cites the exact source page** — verify in seconds, defensible.
- **~1 hour turnaround** vs. days/weeks of manual review.
- **HIPAA, SOC 2, and ISO 42001 certified.** NVIDIA Inception member.
- **First case review is free. No credit card required.**

This is a **B2B software/tool**, not a done-for-you records service and not a
consumer product. The landing page intentionally qualifies for this.

### ✅ Medical providers / experts (primary)

| Use case | Notes for ad targeting |
|---|---|

| Life Care Planning | Comprehensive treatment/cost record analysis |
| Medical Exam (IME) | Objective, organized evidence review |

**Volume signal collected:** cases handled per month (0–10, 10–50, 50–500, 500+) —
useful for distinguishing solo/small firms from high-volume buyers.

### ❌ Individuals / claimants (disqualified)
Claimants are **off-ramped**, not converted. Messaging: *"Superinsight works through
your attorney or provider… not a service for individuals directly."*
**Implication for ads:** avoid consumer/claimant intent keywords (e.g. "how do I get
my disability approved," "free disability help"). Negative-keyword these out.

## 5. The offer & conversion action

- **Primary CTA:** "Get My Free Review" → multi-step qualification form.
- **Secondary CTA (success):** "Start Now — Skip the Wait" (self-serve signup).
- **Promise:** a free first case review; a specialist follows up within one business
  day, no sales pressure.
- **Reassurance bar:** *HIPAA compliant • No credit card required • First review free.*
- **Conversions only count qualified leads** (attorney or medical expert). Claimants
  are routed away and do not fire ad/pixel conversions — keep this in mind when
  optimizing toward conversions.

## 6. Message-match guide for ads

Keep ad copy aligned to the page's own language so the click-to-landing experience
is seamless.

### On-page phrases to mirror in ads
- "Faster medical record reviews & chronologies"
- "AI-built, page-cited summaries"
- "No humans ever read the records"
- "Your first case review is free"
- "~1 hour turnaround" / "review thousands of pages in minutes"
- "Built for attorneys and medical experts"

### Headline angles (by intent)
- **Speed:** "Medical Records Reviewed in 1 Hour" / "Chronologies in Minutes, Not Days"
- **Trust/defensibility:** "Every Finding Cites the Source Page" / "Page-Cited Medical Chronologies"
- **Privacy:** "AI Medical Record Review — No Human Ever Reads PHI"
- **ROI:** "Cut Record Review Time 70%" / "Replace Manual Chart Review"
- **Offer:** "Your First Case Review Is Free" / "Free AI Medical Record Review"

### Description angles
- "AI medical record review & chronologies for attorneys and medical experts.
  Page-cited, HIPAA-compliant, ~1-hour turnaround. First case free."
- "Stop drowning in records. Get a structured, source-cited summary in about an hour.
  No credit card. HIPAA, SOC 2 & ISO 42001 certified."

### Keyword themes (qualified, B2B intent)
- medical record review software / service for attorneys
- medical chronology software / AI medical chronology
- AI medical record summary for law firms
- personal injury / med mal / mass tort medical record review
- SSA / VA / workers' comp medical record review
- IME / expert witness / life care planning record review
- medical record review for [practice area] law firms

### Negative keyword themes (disqualify claimants & wrong intent)
- "free disability lawyer," "how to win my VA/SSDI claim"
- "get my medical records," "request medical records" (patient intent)
- "medical billing," "medical coding jobs," "transcription"
- "nursing / RN jobs," generic "medical records" consumer queries
